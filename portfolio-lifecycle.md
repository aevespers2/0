## Portfolio release and deployment gates

Structural or focused CI does not authorize release when scope, retained artifacts, independent review, upstream contracts, or rollback remain incomplete. Any future deployment must begin with bounded non-mutating/manual validation, record exact source identity and permissions, retain health and provenance artifacts, exercise rollback, and perform post-deployment smoke, integrity, observability, revocation, and disablement checks.
